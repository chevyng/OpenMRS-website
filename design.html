System Design Overview

The OpenMRS server will work as the central database storage where all user's data will be stored, and where the healthcare provider will pull the live data from.

Final Prototype Design

After meeting up with our client, Alex, we went through our individual prototype and after some discussion, he decided that he wanted the layout of the app to be something similar to Citymapper, where there are three main tabs that allows the user to swipe to navigate between pages.

The final prototype design as specified by our client after consultation of our individual prototypes

First Individual Prototypes

We decided to create an individual prototype for our Android app to show our client in order to get feedback from them.
