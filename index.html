Introduction

The objective of this project is to create a health and sports data science platform using the open-source, OpenMRS platform, to gather data from wearable devices and health platform aggregator into a central database where analytics can be performed.

For our project, we will bring the scope of analytics down to the prevention of hypertension.

We will be working with UCL and Bupa to create a health and sports analytics platform that would allow sports, health and academics researchers to easily access the real life data provided by individual users and patients, to perform better world class research in the UK.

This project will be a success when we are able to store an array of health and sports data to our OpenMRS platform and perform analytics on the data.

This project is a vision for integrating UK Sports and Professional clubs into one main electronic sports recording platform.
